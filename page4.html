TOURNAMENT RULES
First 90 Registrations are only allowed.
Only mobile phones are allowed, no Emulators or Tablets.
Teaming with other players will result in permanent ban from Tournaments and may result in Ban from Game for 7-day period.
Players must use the PUBG Mobile Username provided in the registration form for the tournament. Any change in username should be reported to support at least 4 hours before the match time.

Unregistered players entering room will be banned from Tournaments.
